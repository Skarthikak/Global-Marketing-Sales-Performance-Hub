## ‚ú® Report Overview

This report provides a comprehensive, 360-degree analysis of global commercial performance. It is structured around three key business questions:

1.  **Financial Health:** Are we hitting targets and maintaining margins? (Page 1)
2.  **Marketing Efficiency:** How effective is our ad spend (ROAS, TAC)? (Page 2)
3.  **SKU & Geo Drivers:** Which product/country combinations drive the most profit? (Page 3)
